What is BrogTalk?

BrogTalk is an audio downloader for BlogTalkRadio.com.


How to download audio from BlogTalkRadio.com?

  1. Navigate to the show you want
  2. Copy link from address bar
  3. Paste in the box above
  4. Click on "Get link!"
  5. Click "Download Now!!!"
  6. If download doesn't start, right click on the play button, click "Save audio as...," then rename and save the file.
